Crystal Collector Game

Instructions:

  • At the start of the game you will be shown a random number.
  • You will see that there are four crystals that you can click on. When you click on a crystal you will add a points to your total score.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

How to win the game:

You win the game by matching your total score with the random number given to you.

You lose the game if your score exceeds the random score.
